Some questions for user research:

What geographical scope? Should your range be a city, a metro area, a state, a region, like "Upper Midwest" or "West Coast", and what is the UI for geography?

How to contact people? From video calls to calendar scheduling. or controlling whether you can be contacted at all, and about what -- these are open questions.

How to work with your history?
Your network is like affective reality, since it changes when you change your profile. What if you want to connect with someone from your "old network"?
